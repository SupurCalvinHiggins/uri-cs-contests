Welcome to CodeClashRAM!

This is a programming competition website designed for college students in the field of computer science who are seeking challenges to solve and learn from. Let me guide you through how it works.

How It Started?

Dr. Calvin Higgins found himself quite bored during the summer, which led him to enlist my help, "Daniel Diaz," in creating an engaging challenge just for you. The challenge involves a seemingly simple task: counting the words in a file. However, the twist is to discover the quickest method to achieve this. Your task is to find the most efficient solution without relying on assistant tools like chatGPT or GitHub Copilot. Instead, make use of your knowledge and, of course, the invaluable tool we all know as GOOGLE.

Getting Started

For a more comprehensive description of the problem, head over to the "Submit" tab. Once you've submitted your solution, keep an eye on the ranking in the scoreboard section. Lastly, if you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out to Calvin. Feel free to ask him as many questions as you need. Happy coding!
